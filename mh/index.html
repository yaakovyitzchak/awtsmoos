Mawgawl Hawoyluhmee
("the world circle")

the Lubavitcher Rebbe asked us to make sure there is at least one minyan of people in the world, together, during every hour of the day, which is learning matters of Moshiach and the future redemption. This will speedily bring the complete and true redemption, in actuality. So select a weekly timeslot where you can put together a minyan of people, or even a few, or even by yourself, to learn matters of the complete redemption.
